About

About US

Welcome to RCA (Rajasthan Cultural Association) at NIT Silchar! Founded in 2023 on the occasion of Rajasthan Divas, our society serves as a vibrant platform for students hailing from Rajasthan to celebrate and share their rich cultural heritage. RCA aims to foster a sense of community among Rajasthani students while promoting our traditions, festivals, and values within the diverse environment of our college. !

About NITS

National Institute of Technology (NIT) Silchar, located in Assam, India, is one of the premier engineering institutions in the country. Established in 1967, it is recognized for its commitment to academic excellence and innovation. NIT Silchar offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various engineering and technology disciplines. Nestled in the scenic backdrop of the Silchar region, the campus is equipped with modern facilities, state-of-the-art laboratories, and a vibrant learning environment. !
